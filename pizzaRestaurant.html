The Great Story

Our Culinary Journey

Organizer, Craig Stephen, opened the first Giorgio's Restaurant in Los Alamitos, California on September 27, 1977. Today, there are 37 areas all through Arizona, California, Illinois, Louisiana, Nevada, Oregon, Tennessee, and Washington.
